What is an ICO?

An Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is a fundraising method in the cryptocurrency world, where new projects sell their underlying crypto tokens in exchange for funding. It's similar to an IPO in traditional markets.

What is blockchain technology?

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that securely records transactions across many computers, ensuring data integrity and transparency without a central authority.
